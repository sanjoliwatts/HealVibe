About Us

HealVibe Hospital Limited is a leading integrated healthcare delivery service provider in India. The healthcare verticals of the company primarily comprise hospitals, diagnostics and day care specialty facilities. Currently, the company operates its healthcare delivery services in Mysore, Banglore and Pune with 45 healthcare facilities (including projects under development), approximately 5,000 potential beds and 150 diagnostic centres.

We research the latest treatment methods and apply them according to strict scientific criteria. In a global study of the 30 most technologically advanced hospitals in the world, its flagship, the HealVibe Hospital, was ranked No.5, by topmastersinhealthcare.com, and placed ahead of many other outstanding medical institutions in the world.
